Stereotype Threats

Speaking of the unspoken

April 24th 2014

Stereotype threat is a very interesting concept. We were encouraged to look into it for DBC week 6 and think about how stereotype threat may impact our lives and specifically at Dev Bootcamp. Throughout my life, I have rarely felt any negative stereotypes being portrayed on me.

It is a fascinating phenomenon that when people feel that they belong to a certain stereotype that they gravitate towards the expectations of that stereotype. As I pursue web development, there will undoubtedly be stereotypes associated with software engineers that I will face. I am interested to see if people will percieve me as differently being a web developer as opposed to a business person.

I will make sure to maintain perspective on stereotypes through DBC, and be aware about how they impact my life and the lives of my teammates.
